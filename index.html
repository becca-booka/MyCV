As stated above, I am very new to the tech world - I only began studying programming earlier this year (2023). I never even thought to try my hand at programming becuase in highschool it was always stressed to us that in order to be good at programming you had to have very good maths marks, and maths was not one of my strongest subject, so that door had always seemed to be closed to me. However, my mom has always insisted that you didn't need high maths marks to learn to code, you just needed to have a good understanding of logic. This still didn't get me excited about coding becuase to me it seemed that maths and logic were pretty intertwined together as concepts and since I wasnt very confident in my mathematical abilities I felt that I probably wasn't a very logical person either. My mind began to change when I watched my mom take part in the HyperionDev software engineerring bootcamp in 2022. I decided to try and do the free trial HyperionDev offered and found that I really enjoyed learning how to code, and that I was also kind of good at it! So at the beginning of this year I signed up for the bootcamp and began my journy towards becoming a software engineer in February! I am really enjoying my self and am looking foward to finding a career in this field!
